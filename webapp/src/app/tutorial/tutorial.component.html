    Configuring Zerodha Broker


    Step 1:

    Select Zerodha Broker in the Broker Information Screen




    Step 2:

    Provide your Zerodha Client-Id




    Step 3:

    Click on button which will generate the Redirect URL and
    Postback URL as highlighted below




    Step 4:

    In your Internet browser open https://developers.kite.trade and login with your
    email account which is registered with Zerodha account. Note: In case you do
    not have account sign-up using same email which is registered with Zerodha




    Step 5:

    Once Logged in Click on button




    Step 6:

    In the Create a new app window do the following steps

    (1). Select "Connect" option, Note that your Zerodha account will be charged total
          credits(1 credit is 1 rupee) that is mentioned
    (2). Give a name for your app, for ex: MyApp
    (3). Provide your Zerodha Client-Id
    (4). Copy and Paste the Redirect URL generated in Step 3.
    (5). Copy and Paste the Postback URL generated in Step 3.
    (6). Click on button




    Step 7:

    In this step you will have the API Key and API Secret available (Marked 1 and 2)




    Step 8:

    Provide the API Key and API Secret generated in Step 7 in tradebullz Broker
    Information (ones marked in Yellow)




    Step 9:

    Read the Terms and Conditions and Accept the same to proceed and click on
    button to save your broker details




    Configuring ChartInk Alert Action


    Step 1:

    You need to have a screener/filter created in www.chartink.com
    the screener / filter looks like below




    Step 2:

    Create an Alert by clicking on button




    Step 3:

    From the Chart-Ink page in tradebullz.in copy your "Chartink connector Link"




    Step 4:

    In the Chartink Alert, In “Notification to be sent Over” select “Desktop/Mobile (web) option




    Step 5:

    Paste the Chartink connector link copied from Step 3 to "Webhook url"




    Step 6:

    It is always recommended to have the Notification to be Unique as shown below




    Step 7:

    Save the Alert and Copy the Alert Name from the highlighted below




    Step 8:

    In your tradebullz account, click on "Chart-Ink" in the left Navigation Panel




    Step 9:

    Now add a Trade Action for your chart-ink alert, Click on button




    Step 10:

    In the "Add Action" pop-up follow the below steps

    (a). Provide “Alert name” same as the one saved in www.chartink.com which was copied in Step 7
    (b). Provide a proper description for your understanding about the Action
    (c). Choose the "Transaction Type" depending on the type of your Alert Long / Short (Buy / Sell)
    (d). Provide the Percent of "Profit" you want to exit the Trade at (for All the stocks traded with this
           Alert they will be exited at the Profit percentage mentioend here)
    (e). Provide the Percent of "Loss" you want to exit the Trade at (this is the Stop Loss for all the
          Trades done for the respective Alert)
    (f). Provide the Quantity to trade (for all stocks to be traded this will be the quantity)
    (g). Click on button




    Step 11:

    Your Trade Action on alert from www.chartink.com is saved and ready to be Enabled now




    Step 12:

    Clicking on Toggle enables the Action and th Action waits for Trigger from your alert
    from www.chartink.com
